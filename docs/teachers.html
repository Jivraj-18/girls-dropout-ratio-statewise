Policy Prescription

Recruitment and posting policy IS retention policy when it changes who girls see in front of the classroom. States with more than 75% female teachers have, on average, 6.9 percentage points lower girls' dropout. This is one of the strongest actionable levers in the data.

🎯

The Four Quadrants

✅ High Teachers + Low Dropout

These states have both high female teacher share AND low girls' dropout. Models to replicate.

❌ Low Teachers + High Dropout

Low female teacher representation AND high girls' dropout. Priority for recruitment drives.

⚠️ High Teachers + High Dropout

Female teachers alone aren't enough here — other factors (poverty, culture) dominate.

💡 Low Teachers + Low Dropout

Achieved low dropout despite low female teacher share — strong policy environment.
