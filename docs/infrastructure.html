Case Study: The Jharkhand Paradox

💡
Jharkhand: From 22.4% to 2.4% — The Best Turnaround in India
Jharkhand doesn't have the best infrastructure. Its toilet coverage (96.2%) is slightly above average. Yet it achieved the most dramatic reduction in girls' dropout in India: from 22.4% in 2018-19 to just 2.4% in 2024-25 — a 20 percentage point improvement. This proves that targeted policy interventions (conditional cash transfers, girls' scholarships, community mobilization) can overcome infrastructure gaps.
