2.5 Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) and Dropout Rate:
2.5.1 The National Education Policy 2020 (NEP 2020) identified universal education as a key priority,
with several initiatives aimed at transforming the education sector and empowering the nation’s
youth. At the heart of the NEP vision to achieve 100% Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) till secondary
level by 2030, it is necessary to measure GER. The Gross Enrolment Ratio (GER) compares the
movement in the long run is of interest. Chart 2.5.1 to 2.5.4 shows the GER for different levels of
education across the States. It may also be noted that GER greater than 100% might indicate presence
of over or under age children in a particular level of education, inter State migration etc.
2.5.2 Drop out of students & Retention:

primary goals of National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
